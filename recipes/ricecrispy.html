Rice Crispy Treat Recipe

Ooey gooey rice crispy treat will never let you miss a beat!

Crack Snapple Pop or whatever these kids say these days. A rice crispy treat will never go out of style.

You can make a variety of rice crispy treats by adding chocolate, fruity pebbles, m&ms, you name it! We will stick to the original rice crispy treat for today.

Ingredients
  • 1 box of Rice Krispies cereal
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 bag of mini marshmallows
Steps
  1. Melt the butter in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Once butter is all melted, add marshmallows and stir consitantly until completely melted.
  3. Once marshmallows are melted, stir in Rice Krispy Cereal until mixed evenly.
  4. Line a 9x9 pan with parchment paper and gently press down the rice crispy mix into pan.
  5. Let cool and enjoy!
