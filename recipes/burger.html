Cheeseburger Recipe and Assembly

Krabby Patty Galore - Get out of here Plankton!

I remember when I was a kid, I would watch Spongebob Squarepants while fantasizing about the day I would be able to endulge in a real Krabby Patty. And then one day, I grew up a little and my dreams were squashed of ever getting to squeeze inside a box television screen as an animated merwoman or barnical gal.

But the dream of enjoying a perfectly crafted Cheeseburger never died... so here we are! I present to you the one and only way to enjoy a cheeseburger.

Ingredients
  • Sesame bun
  • 1 quarter pound 100% angus beef patty
  • 1 1/2" sliced crisp and juicy tomato
  • 1 slice of Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 large crisp leaves of lettuce off of a fresh lettuce head
  • 2 slices of dill pickle
  • 2 rings of red onion
  • 1 tbsp of Duke's mayo
  • Ketchup and Mustard(optional)
Steps
  1. Smear mayo on both sides of sesame bun and then grill face down on high heat for 2 minutes.
  2. Grill beef patty until medium cook temperature.
  3. Starting from the the bottom bun and working our way up, assemble as so:
    • bottom bun
    • pickle
    • beef patty
    • cheese
    • lettuce
    • red onion
    • tomato
    • top bun
