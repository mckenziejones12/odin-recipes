Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

The most mouth watering chocolate chip cookie ever!

It's the sweet and salty you've desired for so long... and the recipe is so easy, a caveman could do it!

This cookie recipe is my "go-to" whenever I am going to host company or when my husband and I have a late night sweet tooth. Well, no more blogging about cookies. Let's get to baking!

Ingredients
  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup white(granulated) sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 cups chocolate chunks
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Mix all dry ingredients
  3. In a separate bowl, mix all wet ingredients including sugars.
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix with mixer interminttently
  5. Fold in chocolate chunks
  6. Put 1" sphere cookie dough balls on pan 2" apart
  7. Bake in oven for 10 min or until desired consistancy
  8. Enjoy!
